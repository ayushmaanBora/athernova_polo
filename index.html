About Aethernova

Aethernova Technologies Pvt Ltd is a visionary technology company building the future across multiple sectors — from AI and robotics to intelligent systems, automation, and deep-tech innovation.

Founded with a mission to invent beyond boundaries, Aethernova is committed to creating meaningful, human-centric technologies that transform industries and improve lives. Our promise is simple: we will always build something useful for humanity.

We are the creators of POLO, the revolutionary AI educational robot, and HAYA-IQ, an advanced AI system for road safety, among other future-forward projects aimed at reshaping how we live, learn, and work.
